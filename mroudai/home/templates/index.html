Teaching was never a goal for myself. However, after I started in 2010 I found myself liking it more everytime I taught. I taught several students in both class environments and one on one settings. I discovered that I like when my students tell me I made something they thought was difficult very easy. I also discovered that teaching has solidified my foundations and made it possible for me to self learn many skills I currently use today. Because of these reasons I have refined my teaching methods to show students how to properly link lessons together and how these lessons can be applied to the real world.
