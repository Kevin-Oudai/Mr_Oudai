Student Evaluations

Since each student learns differently an evaluation is given to each student before determining how much time they need and where they should start from with the sessions. The evaluation helps to identify how students think and assesses the the level of knowledge they have. Once sessions start I will keep track of the topics they learn and how many topics are required to be covered. After every five sessions they will be evaluated again to determine their progress. In some cases repeated evaluations will not be needed if there is a noticeable improvement in their grades.
